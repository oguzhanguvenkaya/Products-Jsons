5. Sıvı Tahliyesi (Ragleleme) — Uygun bir PPF raglesi (squeegee) ile merkezden dışa doğru hareketlerle folyo altındaki sıvıyı tamamen tahliye edin.
6. Kenar Sabitleme — Kavisli ve gerdirme gerektiren kenarlarda işlemi tekrarlayın ve yapışmanın başladığından emin olun.

## Flexible Polyurethane PU Material

SGCB squeegees are made from high-quality Polyurethane PU, offering the perfect balance of hardness and flexibility. This material ensures that the squeegee can conform to various surfaces without damaging the paint or film.

[

](javascript:;)

## 85-Degree Bevel Design

The unique bevel design allows for easy squeegeeing of anti-slip fluids and water stains, making it ideal for removing air bubbles quickly and efficiently.

[

](javascript:;)

## Magnetic Attachment

Equipped with a magnet, these squeegees adhere securely to the car body, making application easier and more precise. The magnet also prevents the squeegee from slipping, ensuring a smooth and controlled motion.

[

](javascript:;)

## Versatile Shapes

Available in multiple shapes including Mini, Right Angle Trapezoid, Rectangle, Fan Shape, and Trapezoid, these squeegees cater to different scenarios, from large areas to intricate details.

[

](javascript:;)

## Versatile Use

The SGCB PPF Squeegee Series is perfect for applying PPF transparent film, window film, glass film, and even for mirror cleaning and wallpaper application. Its versatility makes it suitable for both large and small areas, as well as concave and convex surfaces.
