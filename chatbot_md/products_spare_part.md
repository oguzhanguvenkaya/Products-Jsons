[How do I choose the right backing plate?](javascript:;)

Backing plates are used with rotary polishing machines. Menzerna offers [backing plates](/car-care/accessories/polishing-accessories/details/premium-backing-plate) in three sizes, depending on the size of the polishing pad. It’s important to remember that the polishing pad should always be bigger than the backing plate.

[What kinds of polishing machine are there?](javascript:;)

There are rotary and orbital polishing machines. Orbital polishing machines are divided into fixed and random models.

[What’s the difference between an orbital and a rotary polishing machine?](javascript:;)

The backing plate of a rotary polishing machine turns in a circle around a fixed point. Rotary machines are ideal for removing deeper cracks in the paint.
The backing plate of the orbital polishing machine produces orbital movements, making the orbital machine suitable for single-stage polishing processes, in particular.

[What’s the difference between a normal and a fixed orbital polishing machine?](javascript:;)

There are two types of orbital polishing machine: random and fixed. The backing plate of a random orbital polishing machine can be turned, while that of a fixed machine cannot.

Frequently Asked Questions

## How quickly does shipping take place?

If your order is received by 17.00, we will ship your order with DPD on the same working day (subject to stock availability). Within Austria, you will usually receive your order on the next working day. To Germany, experience shows that it takes two working days.

As soon as your order has been packed, you will receive an email with a tracking number. This way, you always know where your package is.

## How much are the shipping costs?

For Austria and Germany, shipping is free for orders over 40€.
The shipping costs are different for each country. You can find the current shipping costs here: [view our shipping costs](/en/shipping-costs)

## Is there a minimum order value?

No, there is no minimum order value for your order. Please note, however, that above a certain order value you may be able to save on shipping costs and even choose a free gift.

## What payment options do I have?

We offer you a large selection of different payment options. You can currently choose from classic advance payment (bank transfer), cash on delivery, credit card (Visa, Mastercard, Amex), PayPal, Apple Pay, EPS, Klarna Pay Now and Klarna Slice It. Depending on the payment method, you may be redirected to the provider's website for processing.

## I received a damaged item. What to do?

Unfortunately, this sometimes happens. But don't panic, just send us an email with photos of the damaged item and we'll take care of an exchange / subsequent delivery immediately. This is of course free of charge for you.

## How does a return process work?

Everyone changes their mind sometimes. You can return any order to us. Please enclose enough information so that we know how to handle your return. If you would like a return label from us, our iBot can help you in our live chat.

Everyone changes their mind sometimes. You can return individual items or your entire order. Please include enough information so that we can identify your order. If you would like a refund or an exchange, please let us know as well. This will speed up the return process immensely.

What happens if I fall below the free shipping limit with the return?

In this case, you will be charged for the shipping costs that would otherwise have been incurred, as we have to treat the order as if you had never ordered the returned products.

## What makes your products special?

Our products are professional products. These are difficult to compare with commercially available care products. Our products are used by the industry in the OEM sector. They are state-of-the-art and are continuously improved. You get absolute premium quality.

## Aren't your products too expensive?

Our products are professional products in premium quality. This means that the products are much more economical to use and require less time. This added value alone makes all products significantly cheaper than supposedly "cheap" care products.

## Why should I use your products?

The materials and surfaces in vehicles are becoming more and more complex and sensitive. Our products have been specially developed for the surfaces and materials. This is the only way you can be sure that you are protecting your surfaces for a long time and not damaging them by using too aggressive cleaners. Well-preserved vehicles usually achieve a higher selling price.
