## How to Use

### Step 1: Rinse the Tires
Before using Gyeon Q2M Tire Cleaner, rinse the tires thoroughly with water to remove any loose dirt or debris. This will make it easier for the cleaner to penetrate and remove any built-up grime.

### Step 2: Apply Gyeon Q2M Tire Cleaner
Spray Gyeon Q2M Tire Cleaner directly onto the tire surface, making sure to cover the entire area. Let the cleaner sit for 3-5 minutes to allow it to penetrate the grime.

### Step 3: Agitate the Surface

### Step 4: Rinse the Tires
