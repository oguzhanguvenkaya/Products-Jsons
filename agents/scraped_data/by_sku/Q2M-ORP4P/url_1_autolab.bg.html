Detailed description

Gyeon Q²M OdorRemover Pad is an innovative product in the form of pads that aims to encapsulate unpleasant odors and completely remove them from any interior using a unique technology. Whether it is a vehicle, boat, plane or home, Gyeon Q²M OdorRemover Pad will help eliminate odors and this effect will last for several months. Using only natural ingredients and alcohol-free, it is ideal for car interiors carrying children or pets, as it does not add fragrance to the treated surface. We recommend using Q²M OdorRemover Pad only after you have first cleaned the interior. 4pcs in a set. How to Use: 1. Clean the interior of the car thoroughly 2. Place the pad in the area with the strongest unpleasant odor. Place it upside down, attached to the hangers, as long as it is close to the contaminated area.
