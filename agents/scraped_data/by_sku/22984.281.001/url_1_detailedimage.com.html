Product Description

Heavy Cut Compound 1000 is a heavy cutting polish or compound that comes from Menzerna, which is one of the world's oldest and most distinguished polish manufacturers. This product was formerly known as the Menzerna Power Gloss but it still utilizes the same great formula but with an updated name and label. This heavy cutting polish is designed to help remove some of your worst surface imperfections so you can have the paint looking it's best again. Generic heavy cutting polishes use large polishing particles to get their cutting power that are loosely clustered. These larger particles almost certainly mean the clear coat will have more haze and marring which requires more polishing afterward to restore a transparent clearcoat. Menzerna uses a very different approach that gives you more cutting power and a fraction of the haze that generic compounds and heavy cutting polishes leave behind. Menzerna uses an ingenious method of using much smaller micro abrasive particles that are identical in size and densely clustered. Having all of these uniform micro abrasives tightly gathered gives you outstanding cutting power to remove deeper swirls, oxidation, light scratches and other surface imperfections. If your vehicle has noticeable surface imperfections and it needs to be restored to like new condition then the Heavy Cut Compound is the perfect product to start with. Whenever you use a heavy cutting polish we typically recommend you follow it up with lighter polish(s) to ensure there is no haze and the surface has optimal gloss. Use the Menzerna Heavy Cut Compound today and say good-bye to surface imperfections and hello to a deep shine.

To learn more about Menzerna Polishes and the 2015 label name change please see the Menzerna Polishing Chart.
