DECONTAMINATION AGAINST TAR, GREASE AND ASPHALT.

BENEFITS OF D3 TAR Cleanser


Innovacar's D3 Tar is a degreasing and decontamination agent that suitable for removing and cleaning tar, resin and glue stains from the car body and windows.

More precisely, it is an innovative solution that does not contain hydrocarbons like the classic products on the market and has the following characteristics:

Tar, Asphalt and Road Dirt Dissolver: This is a water-based degreaser designed to pick up road debris and clean it by dissolving stubborn and oily dirt to aid in the cleaning process.

Automotive Resin Remover: It is also effective against organic contaminants such as tree resin.

Suitable for removing adhesive and tape residues from car bodies: it is ideal for post-sticker application, tape removal or for removing adhesive residues from wrapping, films (including PPF) or for removing adhesive residues on windows or bodywork.

Slow evaporation = longer effect: thanks to the water base, the product evaporates longer than solvent-based solutions on the market. This allows the cleaning formula to work longer on dirt stains, making the cleaning process more effective and deeper.

Gentle on surfaces: D3 is completely free of petroleum distillates and thus preserves treated surfaces, making it suitable for surfaces with nanotechnological protection. Thanks to its unique formula, it ensures a better adhesion of the coating molecules, ensuring a longer protection.

Average consumption: 100 ml per vehicle.

Technical data:

Cleaning action: 5/5
Tar decontamination: 5/5
Resin decontamination: 4/5
Glue decontamination: 4/5
Surface safety: YES

D3 TAR IS A PERFECT FIT


Clean the bodywork and windows from tar and asphalt stains, resin, glue and road contamination.
Decontaminate and clean the surface, avoiding interaction with the coating or nanotechnological protection process that has already been applied or will be applied.
Achieve deep cleaning, reducing the need for mechanical action and excessive friction.
Treat bodywork and fragile vehicle surfaces such as rubber and hard plastics with care.
Deep decontamination, but with a safer solution than those on the market based on hydrocarbons.

HOW TO USE D3 TAR


How to remove tar from bodywork, glue and adhesive residue and resin from windows and vehicle with D3 TAR:

Spray the product directly onto the stain to be removed and continue over the panels to prevent the product from drying.
Wait for the product to react for 2 to 5 minutes.
If the dirt is persistent, wipe with a microfiber cloth until it is completely removed or rinse with plenty of water.
For stubborn stains, repeat the process until they are completely clean.

Use : use the product undiluted.

Precautions : Do not allow the product to dry and do not use in the sun or on hot surfaces. Used for windows and painted body parts.
