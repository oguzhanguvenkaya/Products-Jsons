D3 Tar is a powerful, water-based degreaser and decontaminator that safely removes tar, resin, glue and road contaminants from automotive paint and windows. Unlike conventional products, D3 Tar contains no petroleum distillates , making it safer for sensitive surfaces.

How to use

🔹For removing tar, resin or glue

  1. Spray directly onto the contaminated areaand work per panel to prevent drying out.
  2. Leave on for 2 to 5 minutesfor optimal functioning.
  3. For stubborn dirt: Wipe with a microfiber cloth or rinse with plenty of water.
  4. Repeat if necessaryfor complete cleaning.

🚨Important tips:
❌ Do not allow to dry or use on hot surfaces.
❌ Not suitable for PPF, wraps or recently sprayed paint.

Specifications

🔹pH value:Neutral
🔹Consumption:100 ml per vehicle
🔹Tar removal:✅ 5/5
🔹Resin & Glue Removal:✅ 4/5
🔹Surface safety:✅ Yes

Characteristics

✅Removes tar and asphalt stains
Effectively breaks down stubborn tar and asphalt droplets.

✅Resin & Glue Remover
Effortlessly removes tree sap and adhesive residue from stickers, films (PPF) and tape.

✅Longer exposure time due to slow evaporation
The water base provides a longer lasting effect than solvent-based products.

✅Gentle on sensitive surfaces
Safe to use on painted surfaces and coatings without causing damage.

✅Deep decontamination without scrubbing
Minimizes mechanical stress on the paint and prevents micro-scratches.

Hazard information

⚠️DANGER
🐟 Very toxic to aquatic life – avoid release to the environment.
🧤 Wear gloves when applying.
👶 Keep out of reach of children.

💡Key Ingredients:
✔Non-ionic surfactantsfor effective cleaning.
✔Without petroleum– safer for sensitive paints.
