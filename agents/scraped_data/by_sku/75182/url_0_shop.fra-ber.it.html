    THE BENEFITS OF LUSTRATOUCH

    Lustratouch by Fra-Ber is innovatively formulated, combining the effects of a spray wax and polish for cars in a single product.

    • 3-in-1 product: due to its unique properties, it protects, polishes and renders the treated car or vehicle water-repellent;
    • Superior shine: it also provides 30% more shine thanks to its superior characteristics;
    • Easy to apply: it is fast and easy to apply with a microfibre cloth and without water;
    • Dirt-repellent: the treated surface stays protected with a dirt-resistant effect;
    • Preserves surfaces: it can preserve plastic and aluminium body parts;
    • Black orchid fragrance: it leaves a pleasant scent after each application;
    • Multi-format: it is available in both a 750 ml and 4.54 L format;
    • Suitable for all vehicles: it can be used on all types of vehicles including sports cars, utility vehicles, vans, camper vans, trucks and motorbikes.

    LUSTRATOUCH IS IDEAL

    • For a great car finish with a multi-effect product that delivers shine, water-repellency and protection in a single application;
    • As a single product for all car surfaces, bodywork, plastic and aluminium parts;
    • To complete the wash with an instant, easy-to-apply buffing agent.

    HOW TO USE LUSTRATOUCH

    We recommend using Lustratouch as a super-fast protective liquid wax and buffing agent.

    Main steps:

    1. Apply 2–3 sprays on a clean and dry surface or on a microfibre cloth;
    2. Spread the product with circular motions until it is fully absorbed.

    Use: use the product pure.

    Precautions: use a limited amount of product and do not apply on warm surfaces.
