Description:

The FRA-BER TEX 600ML Active Foam is a professional interior cleaner designed to deep clean, brighten, and refresh fabric and carpet surfaces inside vehicles.

Its high-foam formula effectively removes dirt, stains, and bad odors while leaving a pleasant, long-lasting fragrance. The fast-drying, residue-free finish makes it ideal for car seats, roof lining, and interior fabrics, ensuring a clean and renewed appearance.

Directions for Use:

  • Shake well before use.

  • Spray evenly from a distance of about 20 cm, keeping the bottle upright.

  • Wipe with a clean microfiber cloth and allow the surface to dry.

  • The wiping action helps lift away dirt and odor-causing residues.

Highlights:

  • Active foam for deep and gentle cleaning.

  • Brightens fabric and carpet surfaces.

  • Ideal for roof lining — does not damage adhesive or fabric.

  • Safe, fast-drying, and easy to use.
