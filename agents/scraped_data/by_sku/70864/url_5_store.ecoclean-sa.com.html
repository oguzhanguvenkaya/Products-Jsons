      Long-lasting Shine and Comprehensive Protection for Your Car's Tires and Rims
      

      FRA-BER's Tire & Rim Cleaner is a specialized product for cleaning, polishing, and extending the lifespan of your car's tires and rims. Formulated in Italy with an effective solution, it removes brake dust, dirt, and tough residues without damaging surfaces, leaving behind a protective layer that prevents oxidation and maintains a like-new appearance.
      

      Main Features:
      

        

      • 

        Superior Cleaning Power: Effectively removes stubborn dirt, brake dust, and road grime.
        

        • 

      • 

        Comprehensive Protection: Leaves a protective, anti-oxidation layer that preserves shine and extends lifespan.
        

        • 

      • 

        Completely Safe: Does not damage paint or polished surfaces; suitable for all types of metals and rims.
        

        • 

      • 

        Immediately Visible Results: Gives tires and rims a clean, shiny, like-new appearance.
        

        • 

      • 

        Advanced Italian Formulation: Designed for deep cleaning with superior care for surfaces.
        

        • 

      

      Areas of Use:
      

        

      • 

        Cleaning and polishing car rims made from aluminum, steel, or alloys.
        

        • 

      • 

        Car tire care to remove stains and old layers.
        

        • 

      • 

        Cleaning inner wheel wells for complete cleanliness.
        

        • 

      • 

        Care for trucks and motorcycles.
        

        • 

      • 

        Use in car wash centers and specialized workshops.
        

        • 

      

      Important Safety Usage Notices:
      

        

      • 

        Do not use the product when tires are hot (e.g., after long drives).
        

        • 

      • 

        Do not use on rims that are dyed or painted with non-original coatings.
        

        • 

      • 

        Clean each tire and rim separately for the best result.
        

        •

      ✓ Economical & Effective: Strong concentration means less product used and better results.
