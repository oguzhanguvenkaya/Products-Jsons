Description

EFFICIENT COATING AND SEALANT REMOVER

Q²M Total Remover is specifically formulated to remove a wide range of substances from the external surfaces of vehicles without causing any harm. It effectively eliminates sealants, old coatings, factory waxes, and everything in between. This makes it an ideal choice for preparing factory-fresh cars before coating or for removing old coatings without the need for extensive polishing.

START FRESH: Q²M Total Remover facilitates the chemical degradation of any coating, ensuring the surface is prepared for the proper application of a coating or any other protective product without compromising bonding on the paintwork. This is particularly valuable for matte paint jobs or when dealing with cured high spots on delicate substrates.

BEST PRACTICE AND PRO-TIPS FROM YVES HEYLEN: For optimal results, use Q²M Total Remover on clean, wet paintwork during the preparation wash. Apply liberally to one panel at a time, and for maximum efficiency, work the product with a dedicated mitt such as Q²M SilkMitt EVO. Allow it to sit for 30 seconds before rinsing off the panel.
