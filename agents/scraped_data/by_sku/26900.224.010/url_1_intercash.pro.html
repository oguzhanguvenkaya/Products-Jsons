Description

Premium pads Menzerna adapt to all surfaces to perfection. This ensures better sliding properties and easier handling because less force is required.
Professional users enjoy a more relaxed job. Inexperienced users work easier.

Premium pads Menzerna are washable. No foaming. Greater sustainability is guaranteed.

The pads of Menzerna can be used on all machines. Orbital, rotating and compressed air.

  

The pad Menzerna Heavy Cut removes scratches, sanding marks and surface defects in combination with the polish Heavy Cut Menzerna (S300, FG400, FG1100, ...).

It is used both with rotary and orbital machines.
It has a safety edge allowing precise work. Its high quality velcro gives it a very good grip.

 

Eliminates scratches, sanding marks and surface defects in combination with varnish Menzerna Heavy Cut.

The special composition of the polishing foam with a strong cut improves the effect on the varnish of the products Menzerna and delivers final results reliably as soon as possible.

 

Diam 150 mm.

Use with a 125 mm diam tray on both an orbital and rotary machine.
