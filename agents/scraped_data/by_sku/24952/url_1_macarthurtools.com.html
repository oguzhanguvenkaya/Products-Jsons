Epoca EPA15APVITON 1.5L A-Type Pro Viton Pressure Spray Bottle

Pro Viton sprays gun ideal for oils and mineral fats silicone-based, animal and vegetable oils, aliphatic hydrocarbons and aromatic hydrocarbons. Very good resistance to weather, ozone, oxygen and sunlight. Structural components of the head made from resistant polypropylene. Features include fast manual depressurization via side wheel (necessary prior to opening the container). Comes equipped with a protective cap that prevents the non-return valve from direct liquid contact extending working life.

Features:

  • Ideal for Industrial Cleaning, Building and Construction, Automotive, Agriculture
  • Very air efficient allowing long spraying time without the need to repressurize. 
  • Large pump displacement pressurizes the sprayer with minimal pumps.

  • Total capacity:1860ml
  • Operating capacity: 1500ml
  • Gasket: VITON
  • Nozzle: adjustable
  • Safety valve: yes
  • Max pressure bar: 3.00
