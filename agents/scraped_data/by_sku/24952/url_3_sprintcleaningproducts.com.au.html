Description

For the fast, hand-sprayed application of strong thinners, solvents, oils and petroleum-based chemicals, such as those used in the Automotive industry.

Structural parts of the head are resistant Polyamide nylon (PA)
This prevents swelling of the head which causes pressure loss.

Features & Benefits:

  • Structural parts of the head in resistant nylon (PA)
  • Safety valve for automatic depressurization at +- 3 bar; it can be used also manually for releasing the pressure, necessary prior to opening the container
  • Equipped with a protective cap that protects the non return valve from direct liquid contact prolonging its lifetime.

Technical Specifications:

Total capacity 1860ml
Operating capacity 1500ml
Gasket VITON
Nozzle Adjustable
Safety valve Yes
Max pressure 3.00bar
Pumping rod in PP

FAQs

What are your shipping details?

When checking out online a live shipping calculator is available from multiple courier services. Orders are dispatched within 1-5 business days from our Sydney warehouse. All orders are shipped with tracking.

Do you offer wholesale?

Sprint Cleaning Products specialise in the manufacturing of cleaning products. If your business requires bulk supply of cleaning products, we can tailor to suit your requirements. We supply two types of bulk packaging which are Bulk Liquids Packaging and Bulk Powders Packaging. We also have the machinery required for filling smaller custom bottles.

Do you offer free shipping?

 Free local delivery (30km radius from factory in Moorebank NSW 2170) when order values are greater than $250.00. Orders will arrive within 1-4 business days. Leave a custom message in checkout for specially delivery requests. If not home when goods are delivered, items will be left at the front door for collection.

Do you offer pickup?

Yes! You will be notified when your order is ready for collection at our store located at unit 1/90 Heathcote Road Moorebank. Have your order number ready and your goods will be brought out to you for easy collection.

Refund Policy

Returns

Our policy lasts 30 days. If 30 days have gone by since your purchase, unfortunately we can’t offer you a refund or exchange.

If you are unsatisfied with your product after first use, put the lid back on and do not use anymore of the product as we will not accept half empty bottles back.


Shipping

To return your product, you should mail your product to: Sprint Cleaning Products, Unit 5/8 Weld Street, Prestons NSW 2170, Australia

You will be responsible for paying for your own shipping costs for returning your item. Shipping costs are non-refundable. If you receive a refund, the cost of return shipping will be deducted from your refund.

Depending on where you live, the time it may take for your exchanged product to reach you, may vary.

If you are shipping an item over $75, you should consider using a trackable shipping service or purchasing shipping insurance. We don’t guarantee that we will receive your returned item.
