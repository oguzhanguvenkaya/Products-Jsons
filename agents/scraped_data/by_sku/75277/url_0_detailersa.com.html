    Product Summary

    Double Skin creates an extra thin breathable film that protects any kind of fabric including convertible soft-top roofs.
    Product Description

    Nanotechnological solution to protect any kind of fabric including convertible soft-top roofs. Double Skin creates an extra thin breathable film that lasts over time and that can seal fibres and prevent dirt from penetrating into it.


    DIRECTIONS FOR USE:
    Spray Double Skin directly on the fabric, 15 cm away and at least two consecutive times after it has been absorbed (once every 24 hours). Fabrics can be ironed after application. The consumption of Double Skin can vary from 20 to 50 ml per m², depending on the porosity of the fabric. The whole bottle allows to protect from about 10 m² to 25 m² of fabric.

    TECHNICAL FEATURES:
    Appearance and color: aerosol
    Odor: characteristics
    Flash point: 100°C
    Solubility in water: not soluble

    STORAGE:
    Unopened and stored at room temperature away from direct sunlight.
