The Softy Series by KL!N Korea is an exceptional line of products that includes wash mitts, wash pads, and applicators. These products are known for their ultra-softness and can be a game-changer when used properly. You will immediately notice the difference when you hold any Softy Series product.

Based on the Softy Series, the KLiN Softy Series HD takes softness to the next level. The pads and mitts are Ivory in color, which makes it easy to monitor what is going onto your paint during the contact wash process. This feature is especially useful for ceramic-coated vehicles, it gently lifts dirt and effortlessly releases it from the fibers. 

The KLiN Softy Series HD has been in development since 2019, and its exceptional quality and performance make it a favorite among car enthusiasts.
