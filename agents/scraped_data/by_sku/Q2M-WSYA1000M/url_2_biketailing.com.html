About this item:

The biggest advantage of Q²M WaterSpot is its safety on paintwork. Due to inappropriate maintenance, water spots may become an issue on fresh coatings, but there are some quality car washing supplies to help with this. You can remove the most undesirable defects by using O²M WaterSpot water spot remover spray without the need to polish and, in most cases, re-apply the quartz coat.

EASY AND EFFECTIVE – Q²M WaterSpot is very easy to use. Before applying, clean the bodywork with a gentle, pH neutral high-quality shampoo and dry the surface. Then spray the water spot remover spray directly onto the paint or on a microfiber cloth and wipe the area affected with water spots. If there is strong staining, spray generously directly onto the surface and wipe the surface a few times with a microfiber. Repeat if required.

BREAKS DOWN MINERAL DEPOSITS – Q²M WaterSpot is specifically designed to break down the mineral deposits left behind from rain water. These deposits, once exposed to the sun and heat will etch in to your clear coat causing water spots. Remove them quickly and safely with water spot remover spray.
