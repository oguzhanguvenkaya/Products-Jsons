Archetype

Epoca A-type pressure sprayers and foamers are best suited for the professional and trade user. Chemical solutions and applications vary, and Epoca A-type pressure sprayers are available in a variety of seal materials, with pressure spray and foaming nozzles, including bulk sprayers that can be connected to a compressor for constant pressurisation and longer use.

A-Type 1.5-litre PRO PA(resistant) VITON Pressure Sprayer

EPA15PROPVITON

For the fast, hand-sprayed application of acid-based chemical products and dilutions that require VITON gaskets and seals, as well as a more resistant spray head (for use with hydrocarbon, oit and petroleum-based chemicals)

*We recommend checking suitability with the chemical you wish to use, by referring to the Selection Guide. Incorrect chemical use may corrode the gaskets prematurely.

  • Structural parts of the head in resistant nylon (PA)
  • Safety valve for automatic depressurization at +- 3 bar; it can be used also manually for releasing the pressure, necessary prior to opening the container
  • Equipped with a protective cap that protects the non return valve from direct liquid contact prolonging its lifetime.
