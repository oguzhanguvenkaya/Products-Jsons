Description

Sprayer Maxi 1

The EPOCA Maxi  Colour Codes range comes in 5 different head colours (White, Yellow, Red, Green and Blue) to help with easy identification of products.

The Maxi  Hand Sprayer with 1L Bottle, comes equipped with NBR Seals (Nitrile Rubber), a good general-purpose seal which works well with hydrocarbons, mineral oil, fuel oil, benzoyl, diluted acids and alkalis.

Other features include:

  • Capacity 1035 (ml)
  • Spray head output (ml) 2.2
  • seal NBR
  • Bottle is printed with graduated scale in mL and in %

Epoca has been designing and manufacturing chemical sprayers and atomisers for a range of household, commercial and trade uses since 1968 in Italy, where the company was started.
