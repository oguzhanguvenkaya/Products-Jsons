    THE BENEFITS OF MULTIFORCE

    Multiforce by Fra-Ber is a powerful and concentrated degreaser for stubborn dirt such as grease and oil, ideal for cleaning car interiors and exteriors and all types of vehicles and surfaces.

    Main features:

    • Cleans stubborn dirt: it effectively cleans the toughest dirt such as traffic film or grease and oil, making it the ideal cleaner for engines or workshops;
    • All-purpose cleaner: t can also be used on stubborn dirt around the home such as outdoor furniture and grease stains in the garage;
    • Fresh fragrance: it leaves a pleasant clean scent after each application;
    • Multi-format: it is available in both a ready-to-use 650 ml format and a 4.54 L format;
    • Foaming trigger: the 650 ml format has a foam delivery trigger for an even deeper and more intensive cleaning action also on vertical surfaces;
    • Suitable for all work environments: it can be used in all work environments, including shipyards, industrial plants, automotive repairs shops, workshops;
    • Extra shine: it has an effective renewing effect with anti-corrosion agents for grease, smog, stains and traffic film;
    • No added EDTA and NTA*: it is formulated taking into account the most environmentally hazardous substances to be avoided, such as EDTA and NTA.

    *May contain traces.

    MULTIFORCE IS IDEAL

    • As a degreaser that can be used to clean both cars and stubborn dirt around the home;
    • For more efficient, rapid cleaning and washing;
    • For effective cleaning of workplaces in contact with oil and grease such as workshops, industrial plants and shipyards.

    HOW TO USE MULTIFORCE

    We recommend using Multiforce as a cleaner and degreaser for exceptionally dirty surfaces.

    Main steps:

    1. Spray the product and leave on for 1 minute;
    2. Rinse with a damp cloth.

    Use: use the product pure.

    To clean light dirt: dilute to a ratio of 1: 3 i.e., use 250 ml of product and top up with water (750 ml) to obtain a 1 L ready-to-use solution.

    For use as an APC (All-Purpose Cleaner): dilute to a ratio of up to 1:10, i.e., use 90 ml of product and top up with water (910 ml) to obtain a 1 L ready-to-use solution.

    Precautions: Do not allow the product to dry and do not apply in the sun and on hot or delicate surfaces.
