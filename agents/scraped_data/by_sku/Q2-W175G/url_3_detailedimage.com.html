Product Description

Gyeon Wax is an extremely glossy wax with outstanding hydrophobic properties. Gyeon used their expertise in coating formulations and packed it into an easy-to-use wax product. The container has about 6 ounces of wax or 175 grams, that can easily do multiple coats on your vehicle. The unique formula includes fluorine and Gyeon Booster, which is a highly reactive topcoat. This combination allows you to roll the product onto the surface and leave behind a layer of protection that is glossy and hydrophobic. The water beading is amazing from this product and will let water droplets just roll off. It is a great stand-alone product but also works great as a topper to an existing coating layer. Traditional meets sophisticated with the Gyeon Wax!

Application Instructions:
  • Make sure to wash and degrease the surface properly for maximum performance.
  • Apply a light and uniform layer over dry paintwork.
  • Leave it to cure for a minimum of 30 minutes before wipe-off.
