Ceramic Spray Sealant
Universal spray sealant for all vehicles

Easy and quick to apply spray sealant with a very high water-repellent effect. The spray sealant protects the surface from environmental influences and deposits and is extremely dirt-repellent. An unbeatable product for every user in all areas of vehicle detailing.

Processing

  • Spray two to three sprays onto the microfiber cloth (blue)
  • Spread with the microfiber cloth on the surface to be sealed
  • Remove residues after application with the dry side of the microfiber cloth
