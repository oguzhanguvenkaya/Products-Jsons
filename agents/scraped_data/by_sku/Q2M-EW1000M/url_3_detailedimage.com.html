Product Description

The Gyeon Eco Wash is an SiO2 infused waterless and rinseless wash solution! This concentrated, eco-friendly wash tackles dirt and grime while offering several ways to fit into your washing routine. Gyeon Eco Wash eliminates the need for a traditional hose and bucket wash. This water saving formula lets you wash your car while minimizing runoff. You can use Eco Wash in three ways: for a quick waterless wash, a more thorough rinseless wash, or even as a drying aid to add shine and water repellency. For an extra touch of protection, Gyeon Eco Wash is infused with SiO2. This special ingredient adds gloss, repels water, and even offers mild self-cleaning properties, so future washes are easier. Using gentle cleaners this formula is also safe for all car paint finishes, including those protected with a ceramic coating. The concentrated formula means a little goes a long way, making it an economical option as well. Whether you are a car enthusiast or a detailer looking for a convenient, eco-friendly wash, Gyeon Eco Wash is a perfect choice.

Dilution Options:
  • Waterless Wash: 1:10 - 1:15
  • Rinseless Wash: 1:256 - 1:150
  • Drying Aid: 1:20

From The Label

Product Description

SiO2 infused Rinseless and waterless wash. Great as a drying aid or anti-static wipe on events. Leaves a slick, glossy and enhanced finish.

  • Slick for maximum safety
  • Glossy and streak free finish
  • Reduce water usage

Directions

Waterless Wash: Dilute 1:10/1:15. Spray liberally on the surface. Wipe in a straight line motion till streak-free finish.

Rinseless: Use 50-80 ml in 12 L of water. Soak multiple MF in your wash solution. Wash panel by panel. Change towels often.

Drying Aid: Dilute 1:20. Spray on a wet surface or in your drying towel and dry the surface.

KEEP OUT OF REACH OF CHILDREN.
