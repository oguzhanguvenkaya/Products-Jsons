          Gyeon Q ²M Cure Matte is a unique, silica-based spray sealant designed for matte finished paints and wraps. It both a stand-alone product providing durable protection and self-cleaning abilities, as well as a spray detailer for ceramic coatings. It increases the depth and intensity of the color without adding any gloss to the finish.

          With regular use Gyeon Matte Cure can also reduce the risk of waterspots or staining forming and etching into your matte or satin factory paint as well as matte vinyl wraps. A single application of Gyeon Q2M Matte Cure can offer greater than 6 weeks of protection.

          Consumption: 30 ml/car
          PH Tolerance: 4-9
          Durability: >6 weeks

          Before using Gyeon Q²M Cure Matte, make sure that your vehicle is clean and dry. We recommend washing your vehicle with Gyeon Q2M Bathe or a similar car shampoo and drying it thoroughly.


          Step 1 - Shake the Bottle


          Shake the bottle of Gyeon Q²M Cure Matte well before use to ensure that the product is mixed evenly.


          Step 2 - Apply the Product


          Apply a small amount of Gyeon Q²M Cure Matte onto a clean microfiber towel. Spread the product onto the paintwork in a circular motion, working on a small section at a time.


          Step 3 - Buff the Surface


          Using a clean microfiber towel, buff the surface to remove any excess product and create a high-gloss finish.


          Step 4 - Repeat the Process


          Repeat the process on the rest of the vehicle's paintwork, working on one section at a time. Make sure that you apply the product evenly and avoid overlapping onto areas that have already been treated.


          Step 5 - Wait for the Product to Cure


          Allow the product to cure for at least 1 hour before exposing the vehicle to water or other environmental factors. During this time, avoid touching the paintwork to prevent introducing new marks or scratches.
