Q1® Premium Masking Tape

Instant adhesion, sharp edges and a bright yellow shade.

Download
Catalog Download
Technical Data Sheet
Become a reseller

Q1® Premium Masking Tape is designed to deliver high performance and excellent results in the most demanding conditions. The product has been improved for UV resistance up to 3 days.

Thanks to its advanced adhesive formulation, it offers high adhesion without leaving residue when removed from the surface. Its balanced structure allows for controlled unwinding, as well as giving it excellent adaptability and extreme effectiveness when used “tape on tape”.

The backing is designed to achieve an accurate profile of paint edges and allow easier work around corners. The bright yellow color allows you to see through the tape for more precise masking lines and allow superior visibility of masked areas.

Features & benefits

  • Conforms well to curved and irregular surfaces without lifting or tearing
  • UV resistance up to 3 days
  • Thin backing designed to leave low profile paint edges and to better work around corners delivering sharp and clean paint edges
  • Specially formulated adhesive for clean and easy one piece removal
  • Performs at high temperatures up to 110° C (30 min)
  • Solvent & Waterborne paints compatible
