Seven day clean removal
Multiple surfaces
Premium Masking tape Indoor is a semi crepe paper masking tape with natural rubber based adhesive. This tape is designed for almost all indoor paint works, so it’s not UV and water resistant. It sticks well to multiple and irregular surfaces such as walls, baseboards, trims, wood, metal.
Premium Masking tape Indoor assures a clean and easy removal within 7 days, without leaving any residue on the application surface or peeling of the substrate. It performs in overlapping the tape, with masking paper and masking film with excellent holding power.
