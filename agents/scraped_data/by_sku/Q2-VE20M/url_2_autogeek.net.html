Long lasting protection for glass!

Gyeon Q2 View is a long-lasting coating that helps glass repel water, dust, dirt, snow, and other environmental contaminants. This unique formula provides crystal clear protection and is extremely hydrophobic, so each drop of water that hits your windshield will bead up and roll off taking any dirt or dust nearby with it. Gyeon Q2 View can be used on any exterior glass including the windshield, side glass, and mirrors, and it lasts up to 12 months!

When you're on the road one thing is for sure: you need to be able to see the road ahead. While glass cleaner may take care of smudges and fingerprints, it won't do anything when you're driving down the highway in a rainstorm! Windshield wipers help, but tend to simply smear things around. Gyeon Q2 View on the other hand actively helps control the amount of rain and soil that remains on your windshield and exterior glass.

Gyeon Q2 View repels rain, snow, sleet, dust, dirt, and other contaminants for an entire year with just one application. Gyeon Q2 View will even cut down on windshield wiper use, prolonging the life of your wiper blades! The extremely hydrophobic formula allows any moisture to roll away, even when the weather gets rough. Since water won't be smeared all over the windshield, your field of vision will be more crisp and even at night, you'll have a much better view of the road ahead.

Directions:

Always work on clean, dry glass. Clean glass with a high quality glass cleaner prior to application.

  1. Use a thick applicator sponge to apply GYEON Q2 Cleanse in a circular pattern
  2. Wipe away any residue with a clean, damp cloth. Allow surface to dry completely.
  3. Using the included applicator, apply GYEON Q2 Repel using circular motions.
  4. Allow to set for a few minutes, then polish lightly with a clean microfiber cloth.
  5. Allow product to cure for a few hours prior to exposure to water.
