Product information

A MUST BEFORE EVERY QUARTZ COATING

Q2M Prep is a necessary supplement for preparing a vehicle for coating application. It allows the elimination of any oily residues, thus creating a smooth, antistatic surface.

STRONG DEGREASING PROPERTIES

Q2M Prep has strong cleansing and degreasing properties owing to which the preparation of the vehicle for any detailing activities becomes much easier and more efficient.

LIFTING AND DISSOLVING RESIDUE ON AUTOMOTIVE FINISHES

In contrast to other degreasers available on the market, it not only lifts oily substances and residues of paste or old wax, but is also very capable of solving them, hence their elimination becomes much easier.

ADVANTAGES:

  • Amazing antistatic properties
  • Irreplaceable at preparation for vehicle’s detailing
  • High quality degreaser
  • Lifts up oily substances and paste/wax residues
  • Suitable for all types of surfaces
  • By repelling deposited dust Q2M Prep facilitates the detailing process

ADVICE ON APPLICATION:

  • When applying, use a soft microfiber towel, preferably with antistatic properties.

PRECAUTIONS:

  • Keep out of reach of children.
  • Seek medical attention if swallowed.
  • As of August 2018, GYEON Q2M Prep will have a stronger smell. This is due to a formula change to meet new EU guidelines. The product is marginally stronger, so you'll see better results, too.
