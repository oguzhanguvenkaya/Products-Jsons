      Description

      IK Foam Pro 2 ALK | BLUE Hand Pump Foamer | Alkaline Liquids

      Please Note: These units all have a minor imperfection on the blue plastic, near the pump handle, this is from the manufacturing process and is more visible on this new blue finish. We show this in our pictures to be transparent. 

      The IK FOAM Pro 2 ALK sprayer is equipped with the highest performance in terms of safety and ease of use. Manufactured for the generation of dry and long lasting foam.

      Designed to be used with alkalis chemical agents with surfactant properties (surface active).

      They are intended for use in different sectors such as cleaning, disinfection and automotive

      Technical information

      • Valve Set to 3 bar (43.5 psi) with depressurization option
      • Useful capacity 1,25 l. - 42Oz
      • Total capacity 1,9 l. - 64 Oz
      • Litres/Min. 3 bar 0,7l/min. - 0,18 gal/min
      • Gross weight 0,7 kg - 1.54 lb
      • Net weight 1,56 kg - 1.23 lb
