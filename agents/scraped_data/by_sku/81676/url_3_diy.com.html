                        Product information

                        Discover the Matabi iK FOAM Pro 2 Handheld Sprayer, designed for optimal performance and ease of use. This ergonomic sprayer features a generous 1.9-litre total capacity and a wide mouth for effortless filling. Its stable wide base ensures secure placement during use, providing you with peace of mind. With a safety valve set at 3 bar and a locking mechanism on the opening system, safety is prioritized in every spray. Color-coded features make it easy to identify the contents of the tank, enhancing your cleaning efficiency. Equipped with three interchangeable mixers in orange, grey, and green, this sprayer allows you to customize your foam type - whether you need wet, intermediate, or dry foam. The foam application improves the contact time between cleaning chemicals and surfaces, making it perfect for cleaning fabrics and upholstery without excessive soaking, thereby preventing mold and bacteria growth. Enjoy greater visual control as you spray, ensuring thorough coverage. Maintenance is a breeze with the Matabi iK FOAM Pro 2, thanks to its tool-free assembly and disassembly. Whether you're a professional cleaner or a homeowner, this handheld sprayer is an essential addition to your cleaning arsenal. Upgrade your cleaning routine today!
