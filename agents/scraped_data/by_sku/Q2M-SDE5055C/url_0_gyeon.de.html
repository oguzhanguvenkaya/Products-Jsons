Description

Gyeon Q²M SilkDryer EVO - The Ultimate Drying Towel for Perfect Car Care

The Gyeon Q²M SilkDryer EVO is an updated and improved version of one of the most innovative drying towels on the market. The special twist-loop microfiber rapidly changed the drying process in car washing upon its introduction. Compared to the previous version, the EVO variant now boasts an even higher weight per square meter.

Enhanced Drying Performance with Innovative Twist-Loop Microfiber

The Gyeon Q²M SilkDryer EVO sets new standards in car care. With its advanced twist-loop synthetic fibers, it offers impressive drying performance and a streak-free finish. The latest EVO version of this innovative drying towel impresses with a higher weight of 530 gsm and up to 50% improved total absorption capacity compared to earlier versions.

The Gyeon Classic for Car Drying

The EVO version is also characterized by minimally reduced edges, making the towel even more functional and safe on complex-shaped surfaces. This drying towel is a must-have for all car enthusiasts who demand the highest standards in caring for and detailing their vehicles. Discover the future of car drying with the Gyeon Q²M SilkDryer EVO and enjoy every detail of your vehicle in flawless shine. Available in two different sizes and as the SilkMit drying glove (link to SilkMit product if available).

FAQ

Question: How do I use the Silk Dryer towel optimally? The two sides are different.

Answer: Both sides are suitable for drying, but the long pile side is the better option. The twisted fibers have less contact with the surface. Lay this side of the towel flat and, using the two bottom corners, pull the towel across the paintwork. The fibers will absorb the water.

The flat back side, with its surface structure, is ideal for cleaning glass.
