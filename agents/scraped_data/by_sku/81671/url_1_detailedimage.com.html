Product Description

The IK Multi Pro 2 Sprayer will help you easily spray your favorite detailing chemicals! When using a traditional spray bottle, you can quickly become tired when having to pull the trigger over and over. With the IK Multi Pro 2, you will have everything you loved about the IK Multi 1.5 Sprayer, with some newly added features! The Multi Pro 2 features a pump at the top of the bottle, allowing you to pump up the pressure and disperse your favorite detailing chemical with the simple push of the thumb trigger. Hold the trigger and the product within the bottle will be dispersed until the pressure in the bottle has been completely released. This not only saves you the effort of multiple trigger pulls, but can save you valuable detailing time as well. This bottle features a safety valve with depressurization and a protective sleeve for the valve chamber. This protective sleeve works optimally with disinfectants, neutral products, and acids. It is also suitable with solvent-based pesticides, alcohols, and oils, solvents and petroleum by-products. The new trigger lock system helps you avoid any accidental discharges and the non-wobble base saves the unit from accidental tip-overs. The two new color caps fit on the end of the hollow handle, which also acts as a storage area for your fan sprayer and your multi-spray tip. Whichever tip you are not using, toss in the handle to keep safe. Pick up the IK Multi Pro 2 Sprayer and save valuable time when applying your favorite detailing chemical!

Important - Run water through the unit and depressurize before storing. This helps stop premature wear on the seals.

360 vs Standard - The Multi Pro 2 360 features a flexible tube, designed to pull chemical out of the unit when used at any angle. The standard Multi Pro 2 does not feauture this flexible tube.
