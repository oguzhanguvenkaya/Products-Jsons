All Purpose Pump Up Sprayer

The IK MULTI Pro 2 has been refined over the years to offer you the one of the best hand held sprayers on the market. Tired of pulling the trigger back and forth, hand cramping up, switch over to a pump up sprayer to get through the job quicker. Wheel cleaning is one of the areas of a car where no matter the quality of the cleaner used, if you don't distribute it evenly over the wheel, areas will be missed. A pump or sprayer gives you much more control over that distribution, a constant even spray allows you to direct the flow to where you need it. Don't waste time or money again on wasted product.

The large funnel top allows for an easy fill and the hand pressure pump or safety value gives you the control on the pressure when in use or storage.

The safety value is set at 2.5 bar, so you can't over pump the unit and damage any sills. For professional workers, the large stable base and large integrate funnel fill can save time in-between use. Choose between two spray nozzle, a cone for more even distribution of product or a fan if straight spraying is required.

What's more the IK Multi Pro 2, Colour-caps for quick identify the internal product and translucent tank ensure you never spray the wrong product.

The Multi Pro is available in 2 larger forms 8L Multi Pro 12 or 6L Multi Pro 9, all designed for maximum strength and versatility with the most aggressive chemicals, specifically resistant to acids.

Features:

  • Valve - Tared at 2.5 bar / 43,5 psi depressurizable with automatic depressurization option
  • Nozzles - Adjustable conical nozzle and fan
  • Useful capacity - 1.5L
  • Total capacity - 1.9L
  • Litres/Min @ 2.5 bar - 0,7l/min
  • Gross weight - 0.70kg
  • Net weight - 0.58kg

How To Use:

  • Depressurise and cleaner after each use.
  • Dispose of chemicals according to legislation & regulations.
  • If the nozzle is blocked clean with water and do not use metal objects.
  • If the filter is blocked clean it with water.
  • Store the sprayer indoors and avoid temperatures about or below 5-30°C.
  • Apply grease to moving parts and seals regularly.
