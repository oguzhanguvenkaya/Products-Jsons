    IK Sprayers | EMKO Industrial (NI)

    EMKO Industrial offers a comprehensive range of IK sprayers, including trigger, pump, and battery-operated models designed for versatile and efficient chemical application. Trusted for their durability, reliability, and safety, IK sprayers are perfect for industrial, agricultural, and commercial cleaning tasks.

    Our IK sprayer ranges include:

    • Multi Range: Resistant to acids and mild alkaline products for general cleaning.

    • Alka Range: Specially designed for highly alkaline chemicals.

    • HC Range: Ideal for oils, solvents, and other specialised chemicals.

    Engineered for professional use, IK sprayers provide consistent chemical application, maximise efficiency, and ensure safe handling across all cleaning environments.

    Shop EMKO Industrial for industrial sprayers, chemical applicators, and professional cleaning equipment built to last.
