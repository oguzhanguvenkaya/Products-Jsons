FRA-BER DEOTEX PLUS AEROSOL 250ML Deodorizing Sanitizing Treatment For Air Conditioning System

https://www.gecegy.com/shop/fra-ber-deotex-plus-sany-italian-deodorizing-sanitizing-treatment-250ml-9097 https://www.gecegy.com/web/image/product.template/9097/image_1920?unique=cc6d512

Description:

FRA-BER Deotex Plus Aerosol is a professional-grade air conditioning sanitizer and deodorizer, designed specifically for car washing centers, detailing workshops, and car care professionals. It delivers a complete hygienizing treatment for vehicle air-conditioning systems, helping to eliminate bacteria, fungi, viruses, and bad odors trapped inside ventilation ducts.

With its high alcohol concentration (over 75%), the formula ensures deep sanitization while neutralizing odor-causing molecules. Tested according to EN 1276, EN 1650, EN 13697, EN 14476, and EN 16777 standards, it guarantees effective disinfection that meets professional car care standards.

The Deotex Plus Sany treatment improves air quality and refreshes interiors, leaving vehicles with a clean and pleasant environment — perfect for customer handovers and post-detailing services.

Directions for Use:

  • Start the vehicle and turn on the AC in recirculation mode.

  • Set the fan to maximum speed and open all air vents.

  • Place the can on the passenger side floor, ensuring it’s not facing the dashboard.

  • Press the spray button firmly until it locks; the one-shot valve will release all contents automatically.

  • Exit the vehicle, close all windows, and let the engine run for 10 minutes.

  • Once complete, ventilate the interior for about 5 minutes.

Professional Tip: Recommended for car detailing centers, showrooms, and fleet sanitization services seeking a fast and reliable AC disinfection solution.
