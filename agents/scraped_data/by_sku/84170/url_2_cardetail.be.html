Description

iK Multi TR1 Sprayer

iK Multi TR 1 Sprayer is a professional spray bottle that can withstand aggressive liquids and most acids! In addition to the popular iK Multi Pro 2 sprayer, this spray bottle was also developed specifically for the detailing world. The included atomizer is highly resistant to all kinds of chemicals. The sprayer is made of the highest quality and is even resistant to most acids!

Empty spray bottle for detailing

The iK Multi TR Sprayer features a clear bottle with level indicator. The spray bottle also has a provision for a private label. The high quality and sturdiness guarantees a very long service life. Moreover, the iK Sprayer is equipped with a good base which prevents the bottle from falling over. The nozzle is adjustable from a straight jet to a misting spray pattern.

Features iK Multi TR1 Trigger Sprayer

  • Professional spray bottle
  • Provision for own label
  • Resistant to the most aggressive liquids
  • Specially developed for detailing applications
  • Limited resistance to acids
  • Not suitable for solvents
  • Capacity: 1000 ml / 1 liter

What products and chemicals can I use in the iK Multi TR 1?

  • The iK Multi TR 1 is ideally suited For following products:
    • Disinfectants
    • Neutral products
    • Alcohols
    • Alkalis
    • Ketones
  • The sprayer is of limited suitability for:
    • Acids
