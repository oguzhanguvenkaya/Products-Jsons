GYEON Q²M TAR REDEFINED

What's so special about Q²M Tar REDEFINED?


Q²M Tar Redefined is a tar remover that won't damage exterior surfaces and is safe for bodywork. It can be used safely on paint, glass, and plastic trim, both painted and unpainted. Thanks to its pH-neutral formula, it's even suitable for removing tar stains from bare, polished lips without damage.

With daily use, a vehicle quickly accumulates tar and asphalt contaminants, especially on high-powered vehicles with aggressive driving. Q²M Tar Redefined offers an excellent solution to this problem. Its LOW VOC formula accelerates the decontamination process and minimizes time-consuming steps like claying. It's a healthier product for the user and ensures that contaminants are quickly dissolved, allowing you to achieve a smooth finish in record time.

With the REDEFINED line, GYEON has taken its renowned chemical formulas to a whole new level. By reworking and refining them, we deliver unparalleled performance, exceptional durability, and ease of use, ensuring our customers have the best possible experience.

Q²M Tar Redefined is not only a tar remover, but also effectively dissolves organic contaminants and adhesive residue. Work panel by panel to prevent the product from drying out. TIP: Prepare a bucket of hot water and use a soaked Q²M SilkMitt to remove contaminants more effectively. Wash the car thoroughly after completing the work.

How do you use Q²M Tar REDEFINED?


Q²M Tar is not only a tar remover, but also effectively dissolves organic contamination or adhesives. Work panel by panel, preventing the product from drying out.


TIP: Prepare a bucket of hot water and use a soaked Q²M SilkMitt to remove dirt more effectively. Wash the car once the process is complete.
