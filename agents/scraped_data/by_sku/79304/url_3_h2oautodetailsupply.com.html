    H20 Coat by Innovacar is the water-repellent and rain-repellent treatment on your car: simple and easy application, even on a wet car, for an instant hydrophobic booster.

    The product enhances the physical effects of:

    • Sheeting: makes the treated surface (the bodywork) incredibly slippery, generating a self-cleaning effect on the car and facilitating rapid self-drying.
    • Beading or lotus effect: the water residues that are deposited form droplets that are easy to remove.

    Once the product is cured with both effects in operation, a self-cleaning action is activated as the droplets that settle on the surface slide off trapping dirt and contamination within them.

    HOW TO USE H20 COAT

    How to make your car water-repellent and facilitate the drying stage with H20 Coat:

    1. Shake the product before use.
    2. Wash and decontaminate the car.
    3. With the car still wet, proceed panel by panel and spray the product at a distance of about 60 cm, starting from the bottom up and paying attention to distribute it evenly.
    4. After a maximum of 15 seconds from application, rinse the car.
    5. Proceed with the water layer technique to take advantage of self-drying and remove the residues. Basically, thanks to a low pressure rinse jet, a sort of waterfall is created that also removes the water present on the surface.
    6. Use a Micron XXL microfibre cloth to complete the drying.

    For H20 Coat in the 100 ml format:

    • Use: use the product undiluted.

    For H20 Coat in the 1000 ml and 4.54 L format:

    • Use: use the product undiluted.
    • For use in summer or in areas of extreme heat: the recommended dilution is 1:1; then use 500 ml of product and add the remaining part of water (500 ml) to obtain 1 L of ready-to-use solution.
    • For maintenance and cars already treated: the recommended dilution is 1:3; then use 250 ml of product and add the remaining part of water (750 ml) to obtain 1 L of ready-to-use solution.

    Precautions: shake the product thoroughly before use. Do not let the product dry on the surface and treat panel by panel if used undiluted. Should the product dry out and leave traces, W1 Quick Detailer can be used to smooth the surface.
