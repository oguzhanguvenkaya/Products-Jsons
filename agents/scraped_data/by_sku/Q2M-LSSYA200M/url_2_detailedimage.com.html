Product Description

The Gyeon Leather Cleaner Natural allows you to gently remove light contamination from your leather surfaces! Dirt, grime and light oily residue is no match for this unique formula. Leather Cleaner does not contain any softening or preserving additives and is safe on all modern types of leather. There are two cleaning options, strong and natural. Strong works best on heavily soiled leather surfaces, while natural is gentler and designed for maintenance cleanings. For added cleaning power, Gyeon recommends using the Gyeon Leather Brush as well. This high-quality formula also helps prep your leather if you want to apply a leather coating like Gyeon Leather Coat afterward. If you are looking to lightly clean or maintain your leather seats the Gyeon Leather Cleaner Natural should be your choice!

Cleaning Options:
  • Leather Cleaner Strong
    • - Designed to clean heavily soiled leather areas.
  • Leather Cleaner Natural
    • - Best suited for lightly soild leather or as a maintenance cleaner.

From The Label

Product Description

Mild and safe, pH-neutral cleaner for all types of modern automotive leather, including nappa and semi-aniline upholstery.

  • Perfect for daily use
  • Safe on all types of leather
  • Mild cleaning action

Directions

Vacuum upholstery before cleaning. Spray on a small area and clean with Q2M LeatherBrush or Q2M ScrubPad. Remove dirt in a straight motion. Do not rub in with the microfibre. Wipe with a lightly damp cloth to remove excess of product. KEEP OUT OF REACH OF CHILDREN.
