Q 2 M LEATHERSET STRONG

Interior / maintenance

Complete leather maintenance set for used and dirty leather.

Q²M LeatherSet Strong offers a comprehensive solution for cleaning and protecting heavily used or dirty automotive leather. It combines Q²M LeatherCleaner Strong with Q² LeatherCoat REDEFINED, enabling deep cleaning and long-lasting protection in a single step, aided by all the necessary accessories included.

Technical Details

Complete leather maintenance set for used and dirty leather.

Q²M LeatherSet Strong restores heavily soiled leather by pairing intensive cleaning with long lasting protection. It prepares the surface correctly, removes dirt and oily residue, and applies a protective layer that simplifies future maintenance.

HOW TO USE
Q 2 M LEATHERSET STRONG

01
Clean the leather using Q²M LeatherCleaner Strong and the included tools.

02
Remove loosened contamination with a microfibre towel.

03
Apply Q² LeatherCoat REDEFINED evenly to the leather.

04
Allow the product to bond to the surface.

05
Let the leather dry before use.
