Product Description

The Gyeon PPF Evo is an advanced ceramic coating that offers unrivaled protection and clarity for your vehicle's PPF (Paint Protection Film) surfaces! This protective coating is engineered with cutting-edge materials and formulation to provide an exceptional shield against the hazards of everyday driving. PPF Evo is designed to guard your vehicle's paintwork from swirls, scratches, road debris, bug stains, and other common sources of damage. What sets the Gyeon PPF Evo apart is the unique composition of fluoro modified polysilazanes, which makes it incredibly durable & hydrophobic. It remains very slick, easy to apply and it can be applied on both glossy or matte film but also on any vinyl wrap surface. It creates a slick, protective layer without compromising the self-healing abilities of the film. It will enhance color on both glossy and matte films without changing the finish as well. To apply, add a few drops of PPF Evo to your microfiber suede and work panel by panel with crisscross motions. Reflected light is key here as it will highlight streaks or smudges for quick removal, leaving behind a perfect surface. In addition to its exceptional protection and clarity, the Gyeon PPF Evo is also known for its long-lasting durability. Engineered to withstand the rigors of daily driving, this PPF boasts excellent resistance against UV rays, discoloration, and environmental contaminants. It maintains its integrity even in harsh weather conditions, ensuring your vehicle remains shielded and maintains its showroom-quality appearance. With proper care you can expect durability of up to 24 months! With its advanced technology, unbeatable clarity, and long-lasting durability, the Gyeon PPF Evo is a great option for your PPF protection needs!
