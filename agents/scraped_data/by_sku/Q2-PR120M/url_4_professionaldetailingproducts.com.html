Product Details

Advanced ceramic technology combined with user friendly characteristics.

 

Q² Primer is an ultra-light finish and SiO₂ primer. It provides superb pre-coating surface preparation and an incredibly glossy finish. Its main aim is to perfect the finish on the paints which are hardest to work with, dramatically reducing the risk of scratch marks during the final wipe off before coating. Q² Primer leaves a smooth and slick surface, ready to coat.

 

CAPACITIES: 120 ML / 1000 ML
