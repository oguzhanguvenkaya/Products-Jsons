Product Description

The DIY Detail Iron Remover helps dissolve harmful fallout and brake dust fast and easy! Iron deposits can be a huge problem if they embed into the surface and are left to sit, so it is imperative to remove them safely. When you use an Iron Remover, it will cling to the surface instantly loosening up these iron oxide particles for safe and quick removal. Just spray the surface, let the formula dwell, and rinse away. DIY Detail Iron Remover utilizes a color change technology that will show you that the Iron Remover is reacting to the contamination. Agitate with a wash mitt or sponge if the contamination is heavy and rinse away once you are finished. After use, it will leave your paint or wheels cleaner than before and ready for you to polish or apply a layer of protection. Rust deposits, industrial dust, lime deposits, brake dust, and other contaminants are no match for this strong formula. Even though it is strong, it is still pretty gentle as Iron Remover is pH neutral and safe on various surfaces. Safely and easily remove iron deposits with the DIY Detail Iron Remover!

Directions: Spray Iron Remover directly onto a cool surface (wheel or paint) and allow it to dwell for 3-8 minutes. Lightly agitate, if necessary, any ferrous particles will dissolve, forming a purple solution. Do not allow the product to dry on the surface. Rinse the product off the surface.

Product Description

Iron Remover is a powerful and effective way to dissolve ferrous oxides from painted surfaces and wheels. Iron remover utilizes color change technology to show you contaminated surfaces.

Directions

Spray Iron Remover directly onto a cool surface (wheel or paint) and allow to dwell for 3-8 minutes. Lightly agitate, if necessary, any ferrous particles will dissolve, forming a purple solution. Do not allow the product to dry on the surface. Rinse the product off the surface.

For Best Results;
Use in a well-ventilated area out of direct sunlight as the product has a powerful and unpleasant odor.
