     KLiN Drying Duo Microfiber Drying Towel

    What you need to know:

    The drying towel that started it all. Made in Korea with premium raw materials, the Drying Duo dries a car faster and more consistently than any other brand I've used. It 's more durable than cheaper towels, and it's now the option that thousands of customers swear by. The original twin-layer twisted loop drying towel, made for those that have 'heard it all before' and need their faith restored in quality microfiber actually is.

    Not a re-brand. No fancy, money wasting colorful bags. Every cent is put into raw materials, processing and a Containment Control System packaging process that gives you a tool for the job that can be used out of the pack.

    What sets KLiN Korea apart?

    *Manufactured in South Korea – Precision manufacturing in their own facility, using the highest-grade, chemically tested materials (70/30 blend) that meet a multi industry compliance standard
    *Unmatched Absorption – Twisted loop, twin-layer design soaks up a full gallon of water effortlessly. No pressure needed—just drop and drag., year after year
    *Long-Lasting Performance – Unlike cheap generic 'catalogue' towels that degrade after a few washes, KLiN towels perform for years without lint or loss of absorbency. No use of desiccants
    *Contaminant-Free Packaging – Pre-washed, air-washed, and sealed using KLiN’s CCS™ (Containment Control System) to ensure a pristine, ready-to-use towel fresh out of the package. CCS packing done at facility before shipping in fully sealed cartons
    *Trusted by Professionals – This towel is made for detailers who demand durability, consistency, and superior results- in the driveway or a professional shop

    In 2016, after years of testing microfiber from around the world, Detail Division LLC became the official US importer and representative for KLiN Korea. The brand redefined my expectations of quality. After hearing years of hearing the claims 'we offer the best microfiber drying towels', KLiN Korea is the only microfiber manufacturer that truly represented my own work with a truly impressive solution. The Drying Duo isn’t just another drying towel—it’s the result of relentless pursuit for superior products.

    Measures 90 X 70cm (35" X 27")
