    The Rag Company is committed to shipping your order quickly, safely, and affordably. Please review the details of our shipping policy below.

     

    Shipping Coverage

    We currently ship only within the United States, including:

    Alaska and Hawaii

    State-side APO/FPO addresses

    We do not ship internationally. If you are located outside the U.S., please visit our Distributor Page to find an authorized reseller in your region.

     

    Processing Time

    Most orders are processed and shipped within 48 hours of being received.

    During peak sale times can extend the time it takes us to process the orders.

    Orders are shipped Monday through Saturday (excluding holidays).

    Delivery timeframes begin once the carrier picks up the order from our warehouse.

     

    Shipping Rates

    Flat Rate: $9.99 for orders under $115 (pre-tax, after discounts).

    Free Shipping: Orders $115 and above (pre-tax, after discounts).

    Rates are applied automatically at checkout.

     

    Carriers & Methods

    USPS Priority Mail (2–3 days) is our primary shipping method.

    USPS Parcel Select or UPS may be used at our discretion if more cost-effective.

    Shipping times vary by carrier and destination; we cannot guarantee specific delivery dates.

     

    Tracking Information

    A tracking number will be emailed once your order leaves our warehouse.

    Please check your spam/junk folders if you do not see the email.

    Incorrect or missing email addresses will prevent tracking updates.

     

    Delivery & Liability

    Most packages are delivered without requiring a signature. Drivers may leave packages at their discretion.

    A “signature required” service is available by request for an additional fee.

    Once a package is delivered to the shipping address provided, The Rag Company is not responsible for lost, stolen, or misdelivered packages.

    Customers are responsible for providing accurate and complete shipping information at checkout.

     

    Questions

    If you have any questions about shipping or need assistance with your order, please contact us at support@theragcompany.com.
