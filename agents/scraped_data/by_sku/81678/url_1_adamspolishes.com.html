Say goodbye to manual pumping and hello to convenience with the iK E Foam Pro 2 – the ultimate electric foam sprayer designed for professional and enthusiast detailers. Built on the trusted platform of the IK Foam Pro 2, this upgraded model features an integrated rechargeable battery-powered compressor that delivers continuous pressure at up to 43.5 psi with no manual effort required.

  • Cordless & Battery Operated
  • Rechargeable - 8 to 10 Tanks Before Recharging
  • Quick & Easy One Touch Power Button
  • Builds Up To 43.5 PSI In Pressure
  • Produces Thick And Clingy Foam
  • Optimal For Water Saving Mobile Detailing

We currently offer free shipping in the continental US on orders over $75 after discounts or $7 Flat Rate Shipping. If you don't love what you receive, we'll cover shipping back to our door too. Please note, free returns are not included on international orders.

With just a 3-second hold of the power button, the IK e Foam Pro 2 activates and begins building pressure with a simple tap. Once pressurized, pull the trigger to unleash a steady, rich stream of foam that stays consistent until the tank is empty. It’s the ideal solution for creating thick, clinging foam quickly—perfect for car shampoos, paint pre-treatment, wheel cleaning and general detailing chemistries.

Whether you're a mobile detailer, working in an area with water restrictions, or don’t have access to a pressure washer or foam cannon, this battery-operated foam sprayer offers the ultimate in portability and performance. Just add your favorite car shampoo or chemical solution and experience fast, even coverage without the hassle of hand-pumping. The iK E Foam Pro 2 transforms your detailing workflow, making it easier, faster, and more efficient than ever before.

Other Specifications

  • Maximum Pressure: 43.5 psi
  • Gross weight (empty): 1.23 lbs
  • Total capacity: 64 oz (1.9 L) 
  • Battery: 7.4v 3200mAh
  • Includes 3 Color-Coded Mixer Adaptors! Your choice of dry, wet, or standard foam.
      • Orange: Watery or Wet Foam
      • Green: More Dry Foam
      • Grey: Standard Foam
