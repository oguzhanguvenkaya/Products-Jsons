Lubricant for the application of PPF films Gyeon Q2M PPF Slip

The Q²M PPF Slip is the latest addition to the specialized PPF Gyeon range. It comes as the ultimate solution designed specifically to work hand-in-hand with Gyeon's protective PPF films. Of course, you can use it with any other film on the market. Its high concentration allows dilution up to 1:100. Just add 10 to 15 ml to one liter of demineralized (distilled) water and you will get a sufficient amount of lube solution ready for immediate use. If necessary, you can adjust the ratio according to your personal preferences.

Main features

  • High viscosity, rapid evaporation.
  • PPF lubricant.
  • Removes haze and stains.
  • Easy wiping.
  • Concentrate.

How to use

  • Dilute PPF Slip 1:100 with distilled water.
  • Shake well.
  • Spray liberally on both the PPF and the installation surface.
  • Continue installing the film.
  • Lubricate continuously if necessary.

About the brand

The Gyeon brand (pronounced "jion") comes from South Korea, where several global brands dealing with high-quality and innovative car cosmetics were created. South Koreans are known to be very precise and hardworking, and these qualities are perfectly reflected in the products of this brand. Just by looking at the design of their products, it is obvious that they have taken care. But pretty packaging isn't everything. This car cosmetic offers advanced technologies at competitive prices. Gyeon develops and manufactures almost every product in its own laboratory and factory in South Korea with the help of the best scientists and experts in the field of car care. They support this process with in-depth research, intensive testing and an obsessive desire to improve everything they do. This approach results in exceptional products in terms of quality, durability and ease of use. Gyeon has always been unique and always strives to be one step ahead and is willing to win in every aspect of his business. Passion and experience keep them on this path. They never compromise. In Europe, this car cosmetic is very popular. Today, Gyeon is sold on 6 continents and in more than 100 countries around the world.
