Lubricant for PPF Film Application Gyeon Q2M PPF Slip

Gyeon Q²M PPF Slip is the newest addition to the Gyeon product line designed for PPF films. It was developed to be compatible with all PPF films from Gyeon as well as other manufacturers. Q²M PPF Slip has a very high concentration, allowing dilution up to a ratio of 1:100. Q²M PPF Slip was designed with efficiency and economy in mind for detailing studios. Thanks to its high concentration (1:100), installation technicians can easily adjust the solution to their specific environment and glide preferences with minimal impact on their daily operating costs. Gyeon added a small amount of ethyl alcohol to speed up evaporation and gently activate the adhesive, enabling quick and efficient installation of PPF film on complex-shaped panels. At the same time, it improves the appearance of the film shortly after installation.

Main Features

  • Lubricant for PPF film application.
  • Highly concentrated – 1:100.
  • High viscosity and fast evaporation thanks to the use of ethyl alcohol.
  • Highly economical – reduces operating costs for detailing centers.
  • Includes a practical dispenser.

How to Use

  • Dilute Gyeon Q²M PPF Slip at a ratio of 1:100 with distilled water.
  • Shake well.
  • Spray generously onto the PPF film and the installation surface.
  • Continue with the film installation.
  • If necessary, lubricate continuously during installation.
