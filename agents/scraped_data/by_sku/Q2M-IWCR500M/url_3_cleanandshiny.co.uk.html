Discover the full range of Gyeon car care products at Clean and Shiny – your official UK stockist for advanced ceramic coatings, detailing sprays, and premium SiO₂-based car protection. Why Choose Gyeon for Car Detailing?

  • Long-lasting ceramic protection for paintwork, wheels, and trim
  • Outstanding water beading and hydrophobic effects
  • Superior chemical resistance and UV durability
  • Easy application, even for home detailers

Whether you're maintaining daily drivers or protecting show cars, Gyeon offers professional-grade solutions with user-friendly application.
