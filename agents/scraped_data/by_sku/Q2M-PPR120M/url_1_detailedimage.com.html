Product Description

Gyeon PPF Renew is an all-in-one product designed to help you polish and protect your Paint Protection Film (aka PPF)! PPF is designed to help you protect your paint and it does an amazing job at that. However, even though it protects so well PPF can still become dirty and dull over time. Renew is designed to clean, polish and protect your PPF in one step. This formula is packed with very fine abrasives to help remove imperfections and bonded contamination deep in the pores of the PPF, restoring the initial slickness and smooth feel of the film. After use, you will be left behind with a protective, self-cleaning layer of silica designed for high repellency, UV protection, and gloss! It's the ultimate all in one product for PPF! If you are looking to polish, clean and protect your PPF, the Gyeon PPF Renew is a great choice!

Manufacturer Estimates Consumption: 60ml/ car

Manufacturer Estimates Durability: Up To 8 months

Professional Detailer Reviews

Product Description

Deep cleansing and reviving effect on glossy PPF of all types. 1-step silica polish and renewer. Adds gloss and depth. Restores the smoothness of the film. Removes light mark and hazing. Leaves a slick, silica based protective layer.

Directions

Apply with an eccentric machine polisher and a soft finishing pad on minimum revs. Work until only a light haze remains. Polish with a soft microfibre towel until full gloss.
