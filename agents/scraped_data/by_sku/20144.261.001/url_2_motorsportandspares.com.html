Description

Menzerna Liquid Matt provides an alternative to sanding. This abrasive paste establishes the conditions required for reliable results in repainting and refinishing.

Liquid Matt is water-based and facilitates subsequent priming/painting of the vehicle. It can be used by hand or machine.

This silicone-free formula is bodyshop safe and leaves an even finish creating the optimum conditions for painting.

Directions:

-         Use: preparing the surface prior to the coating process. The product is a substitute for sandpaper when matting surfaces

-         Apply Liquid Matt to a wet abrasive pad and buff the surface in circular motions

-         Rinse away any residue with water

Size: 1 Litre
