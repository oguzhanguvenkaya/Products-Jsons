Description

Q2M Seatcover

"Protect your vehicle's front seats from dirt, spills, and grime during detailing with the GYEON Q²M Seatcover. This essential accessory provides a simple yet effective barrier, ensuring your seats remain pristine while you work. Designed for easy use, the seat cover slips on effortlessly and is secured with a reliable elastic band, offering a snug and secure fit. Reusable and durable, the GYEON Q²M Seatcover is a must-have for any detailer looking to maintain a clean and professional workspace.  

Why Choose the GYEON Q²M Seatcover?

  • Essential Seat Protection: Shields front seats from dirt, spills, and grime during detailing.  
  • Easy Application: Slips on quickly and securely with an elastic band.
  • Reusable and Durable: Built to withstand repeated use, providing long-lasting protection.
  • Universal Fit: Designed to fit most standard front seats.
  • Maintains Cleanliness: Helps keep your vehicle's interior clean and professional.

Key Features

  • Protects front seats during detailing.  
  • Elastic band attachment for secure fit.
  • Reusable design.
  • Single-piece construction.

Pro Tip

  • Ensure the seat is relatively clean before applying the cover to prevent trapping dirt.
  • Adjust the elastic band for a snug fit to prevent slippage during movement.
  • After use, wipe down the seat cover with a damp cloth to remove any detailing product residue.
  • Store folded, to keep the cover clean and take up minimal space.
