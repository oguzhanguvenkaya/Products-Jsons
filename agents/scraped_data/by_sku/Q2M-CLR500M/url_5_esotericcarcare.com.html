Gyeon ClayLube is extremely slick which reduces friction and helps to prevent the clay from ‘grabbing’ and marring the surface.

Ideally used with Gyeon Clay for best results, Clay Lube can be used with any clay bars, cloths or discs to lubricate very well. Some marring may occur, so plan to perform paint correction after using Q2M Clay and Q2M ClayLube Redefined.

Gyeon ClayLube Redefined can be diluted down as far as a 1:10 ratio, which offers great economy, without impacting its high performance and still allowing good lubrication for the clay to do its job. ClayLube is very dense, so only small amounts of the product are required to get the right lubrication.

ClayLube Redefined is totally neutral and developed to work with any brand of clay bar. It does not affect the clay and remains safe for the most delicate bars. At the same time it does not cause any staining on the paint.

Contents: 500ml
