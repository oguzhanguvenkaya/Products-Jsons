Innovacar S1 Wash & Coat 1L - shampoo with SiO2

Innovacar S1 Wash & Coat - the perfect product to achieve many effects in one: washes and cleans, seals and protects, gives shine and leaves a hydrophobic effect. It is the ideal advanced cleaning solution, reducing steps when washing or servicing a car with a ceramic coating or other protection.

Product features:

  • 3 in 1 car shampoo: it is the perfect nanotechnology solution that will shorten the necessary stages of the washing cycle, because it cleans, seals and leaves a hydrophobic effect; S1 Wash&Coat combines washing and protection steps in one.
  • Coating maintenance and nanotechnology: this is the ideal product to use on weekly dirt to revive nanotechnology and prolong the performance of a previously applied coating.
  • SiO2 protective shield: the shampoo adheres to the surface of the car body, creating a protective barrier that protects it against alkaline agents and dirt. Extends and protects the life cycle of the coating and previously used nanotechnology and ceramic protections.
  • UV protection: the created barrier protects the surface against UV rays.
  • Water repellent effect: provides a hydrophobic and slippery effect that lasts up to 4 months.
  • Deep organic decontamination: The product has been enriched with patent-protected enzymes for deep organic decontamination; therefore, it can act several times on the organic dirt particles of the substrate.
  • High concentration: Significant and thorough cleaning and washability is guaranteed even with small amounts of S1 Wash&Coat.

Average consumption:

In case of application with a foam sprayer or bucket: the recommended dilution is from 1:50 to 1:80, i.e. use 20 ml to 10 ml of the product and fill the rest with water (980 ml - 990 ml) to obtain 1 L of solution ready for use. use.

In case of application with a foam lance or foam gun: the recommended dilution is 1:3, i.e. use 250 ml of the product and fill the remaining part with water (750 ml) to obtain 1 L of ready-to-use solution. When dosing, the actual final dilution may reach approximately 1:60.

For maintenance cleaning of seals or coatings: the recommended dilution is from 1:80 to 1:150, i.e. use 10 ml to 7 ml of the product and fill the remaining part with water (990 ml - 993 ml) to obtain 1 L of ready-to-use solution.

  Wait up to 7 hours after application for full activation. The effect lasts up to 4 months.
    W1 Quick Detailer can be used to remove marks after the product has dried.
