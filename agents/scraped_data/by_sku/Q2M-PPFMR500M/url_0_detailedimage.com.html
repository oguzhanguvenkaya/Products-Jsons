Product Description

The Gyeon PPF Maintain provides essential spray on protection against harmful environmental factors for your PPF (Paint Protection Film) surfaces! With an innovative formula, Maintain creates a hydrophobic barrier that repels water, dirt, and other pollutants, effectively reducing the risk of staining or damage to your PPF. This protective layer also enhances the self-cleaning properties of your PPF, making cleaning even easier in the long run. By regularly using the Gyeon PPF Maintain, you can effortlessly maintain a pristine and flawless look. To apply, simply spray the product onto the surface of your PPF, ensuring an even coverage. Using a clean microfiber towel gently wipe away any excess, leaving behind a refreshed and glossy finish. With its user-friendly application, the Gyeon PPF Maintain is perfect for both enthusiasts and professionals alike. Maintain is designed to complement and enhance the longevity of your existing PPF. It works seamlessly with Gyeon's range of PPF products, ensuring a comprehensive and integrated care routine. Whether you have recently installed a PPF or are looking to revitalize an older one, Maintain is your go-to solution for long-lasting protection and stunning visual appeal. Choose the Gyeon PPF Maintain and experience the pinnacle of PPF care and maintenance!

Suitable for matte PPF: Yes
PH Tolerance: 4-9
SIO2 Percentage: >5%
Contact Angle: >80'
Consumption: 30ml/ car

From The Label

Product Description

High-performance PPF sealant & maintenance spray. Suitable for any PPF. Adds depth, slickness and self cleaning.

  • Ceramic PPF maintenance
  • Self-cleaning properties
  • Water repellent

Directions

Shake well before use. Use only on clean surface. Spray onto a microfiber and wipe a panel at a time till a streak free finish. Do not spray directly on PPF. Wet/dry use possible, also as a stand alone product. Works great as a drying aid.
KEEP OUT OF REACH OF CHILDREN.
