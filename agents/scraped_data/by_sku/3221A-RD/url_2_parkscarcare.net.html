KLIN Softy Series Wash Mitt | Ultra Soft Car Wash Mitt

The Softy Series Wash Mitts are hands down the best wash media we have ever used. These products are made of premium ultra-soft and plush microfiber that effortlessly glides across paint, and releases dirt easily. They will help to prevent marring or scratching even the most delicate of surfaces during the hand washing process. Not to mention they hold up amazing over time!

Mitt Size: 8" X 6"

Washing:

We advise using Gyeon Quartz Q2M Towel Wash to maintain the longevity and performance of KLIN microfiber products.

Machine wash separately using neutral detergent with warm water and hang dry. 

DO NOT use fabric softeners or bleach.
