Gyeon Q² Leather Coat Redefined 500ml

The easy to apply leather protective coating for all leather interiors including seats and steering wheels.

Description

Gyeon Q² LeatherCoat Redefined is user-friendly; clean the leather first for a strong bond, then apply with a soft microfiber cloth or applicator in an even layer.

This hydrophobic sealant lasts up to 3 months, protecting against liquids, sweat, dirt, and light dye transfer from leather.

Strong protection & matte look, one layer of Q² LeatherCoat Redefined offers excellent protection, being hydrophobic and repelling liquids and dirt. Offers UV protection, preserving colour freshness in hot, sunny conditions.

Q² LeatherCoat Redefined shields leather from dirt, sweat, salt, and fats. Hydrophobic properties and strong surface bonding protect against pollutants penetrating trim structure. Most modern automotive leather has a factory matt finish. Gyeon Q² LeatherCoat provides an invisible layer that preserves the look and feel of soft leather trim. It coats the leather to keep it looking new.

 

How to use Gyeon Q² Leather Coat Redefined
  1. Apply an even layer of Gyeon Leather Coat
  2. Allow to dry for 2-3 minutes
  3. Buff with a microfibre towel.
