Lava Interni B Aroma

Intensive detergent for fabrics patented** with enzymes

Lava Interni B Aroma from Evoenzymes line is an innovative scented product to clean your car’s fabric surfaces: it combines the cleaning action of the traditional “Lava Interni B” with the exclusive enzyme-based technology that compacts fibres and eliminates organic odors. It removes all residues from the fabrics, with a quick self-drying effect.

The main features:

  • Patented** formula with added enzymes to improve the quality of car cleaning.
  • It cleans fabrics thoroughly and immediately.
  • Longer-lasting cleaning thanks to the removal of organic residue.
  • Drastic odour control, thanks to the complete biodegradation of organic dirt molecules.
  • A wonderful scent of fresh clean will amaze your customers.
  • No NTA/PHOSPHOROUS/POTASSIUM HYDROXIDE added*.
  • Contains surfactants that meet the biodegradability criteria according to Regulation (EC) 648/2004.

* May contain traces.
** Patent no. MI2013A000782.

Read more

Directions for use

Spray system: 1:25-1:50

Pre-dilution

The product can be pre-diluted.
Storage method and water quality may influence the shelf life of the pre-diluted solution.

Warnings

When handling the product, observe the advice given in the MSDS and data sheets, the manufacturer’s warnings on the devices and materials involved and, if necessary, check for tolerability. Keep container closed and only use plugs with tube passages. The raw materials used may lead to changes in colour and turbidity, without this leading to a drop in performance and/or quality. In the case of high water temperature (> 40°C) and/or water hardness (> 4.5 mmo/L ≈ 25° dH), limescale formation can be prevented by adding “Ca Clean”.
