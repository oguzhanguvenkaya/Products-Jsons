Product Description

The Gyeon Syncro Evo is a unique coating system designed to provide long-lasting performance, protection and self-cleaning properties! Most coating manufacturers offer coatings and other separate products you can use as a topper to enhance hydrophobic properties, add some gloss, and help keep the coating layer protected and performing it's best. With the original Syncro, Gyeon came up with an easy to use, two step coating process that includes everything from application materials, to the coating, to the topper. With Syncro Evo, you will have everything you loved about Syncro, but enhanced for increased durability, gloss, and easier application. After thoroughly cleaning, polishing and prepping your paint, you will want to apply the included Mohs Evo, followed by Skin Evo and finally topped with the Cure. This three step, 3-4 layer application will greatly enhance gloss and leave you with a durable layer of protection for up to 50 months. Future cleanings will be much easier as less contamination will stick to the paint and for the dirt and grime that does, it will be easier to remove during your wash process.

Gyeon coatings have been completely redesigned and this technology has never used before in car coatings. Evo products utilize a world-first use of the fluoro-modified polisylazane technology that pushes the Evo line to the forefront of car coatings. This technology means extended durability, extreme chemical resistance, longer lasting performance, and only one layer is needed. It remains easy to apply and much easier wipe off than before.

If you want to step up your coating game, give the Gyeon Syncro Evo a try!

Note: All Evo formulas can be applied outdoors. Avoid direct sun and ideally do not get wet in the first 12 hours after application.
  • Consumption: 15-25ml/car
  • PH Tolerance: 2-11
  • Durability: up to 50 Months / 50KKM
