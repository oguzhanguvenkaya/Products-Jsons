FRA-BER KO IRON 4.54L

Car iron remover designed to eliminate iron and mineral residues that are challenging to remove with regular cleaners.

  • Field of application: various car surfaces such as rims, bodywork and windows.
  • Effectively eliminates iron deposits from brake pads, city pollution, rail dust, and acid rain.
  • Colour indication: it turns purple when it contacts iron micro-particles and rust.
  • Gel formula: highly concentrated for better adhesion and performance on vertical surfaces.

Ko Iron by Fra-Ber is a specialized car iron remover designed to eliminate iron and mineral residues that regular cleaners struggle to remove. It is suitable for use on various car surfaces, including rims, bodywork, and windows.


The product efficiently removes iron deposits from brake pads, city pollution, rail dust, and acid rain. It features a unique colour-changing effect that turns purple when it comes into contact with iron micro-particles and rust, providing a visual indication of its action.


Application tips: For rust, use the product in its pure form. For surfaces contaminated with iron, dilute it to a ratio between 1:1 and 1:2.

Do not allow the product to dry and avoid applying it in direct sunlight or on hot surfaces.
